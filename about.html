What is Ayurveda?

Ayurveda is an ancient system of medicine that originated in India over 5,000 years ago. It focuses on natural healing through herbal remedies, diet, yoga, and holistic wellness.

Principles of Ayurveda

Ayurveda is based on three doshas (Vata, Pitta, Kapha) which influence a person’s body, mind, and health.

Benefits of Ayurveda

Ayurvedic treatments improve digestion, immunity, mental health, and overall well-being.
